## The idea (one paragraph)
Chaka Priority Atlas is an AI-powered natural restoration prioritization tool for Ethiopia that helps NGOs, investors, and government stakeholders decide which geographic areas should receive restoration funding first. The demo lets users choose South Ethiopia or Southwest Ethiopia, select geospatial evidence layers, run a mock analysis, inspect a dashboard with ranked restoration areas, understand why a specific area is recommended, review the suggested intervention package, and preview an exportable project brief.
