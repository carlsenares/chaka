This ecosystem does not have the capability to store carbon, since the formed

The value of these ecosystems is the temporary nutrient fixation, the
importance as habitat for aquatic fauna and birds and its attractiveness and
ornamental use for tourists.
The natural siltation zones are threatened by grazing. In areas where cattle can
reach the floating leaves they will consume the whole stand.
In general those areas should be protected. Some untouched areas can be
secured as core zones, whereas areas used for grazing should be protected as
buffer zones. A further touristic utilization has to be prevented.
