b. Daily Calendar

Daily Calendar of Awulo Village - Men group

September
Time                     Activities
4:00 – 6:00              People wake up at about 4:00am in the night to sharpen their
                         plow (visit the iron smith), and then come back home to feed
                         their oxen.
6:00 – 7:00              Breakfast
7:00 – 12:00             Plowing, harvesting, and cutting grass as required
12:00 – 13:30            Lunch, drinking coffee, and feeding oxen
13:30 – 18:00            Plowing, harvesting, and cutting grass as required
18:00 – 20:00            Feeding oxen and cows
20:00 – 21:00            Dinner and chatting with family members
21:00 – 22:00            Sleeping

October
Time                     Activities
3:00 – 9:00              People wake up at 3:00am in the night for harvesting. Mowing
                         is mainly during the night time and continues until 9:00 am
9:00 – 10:30             Breakfast, and coffee
4:30 – 18:00             Gathering harvested crops, hand threshing for immediate food
                         consumption, and feeding animals. Food is served at the work
                         places (farm lands) to save time.
18:00 – 20:00            Back home, feed cattle
20:00 – 21:00            Dinner and chatting with family members
21:00 – 22:00            Sleeping

November
Time                     Activities
5:00 – 9:00              People wake up at about 5:00 am in the morning to clean
                         threshing field, gather oxen for threshing from oneself and from
                         others (known locally as ‘lifinti’), and feed oxen. Breakfast and
                         coffee is served at the threshing field.
9:00 – 14:00             Threshing continues, lunch as well as local beer (‘sua’) is
                         served in the field.
14:00 – 19:00            Winnowing, separating the produce from straw, and gather what
                         is available. If job not completed it continues into the night
19:00 – 21:00            Back home, take half shower (particularly face and head),
                         chatting with family, and dinner
