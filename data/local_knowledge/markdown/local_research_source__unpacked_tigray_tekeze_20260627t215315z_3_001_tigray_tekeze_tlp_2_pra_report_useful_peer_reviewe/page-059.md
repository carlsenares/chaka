October
Time                    Activities
4:00 – 12:00            People wake up at 4:00 am in the night for harvesting teff.
                        Breakfast is served in the field. Harvesting continues till mid
                        day.
12:00 – 14:00           Lunch
14:00 – 16:30           Harvesting continues
16:30 – 18:00           Gathering what is harvested (‘mine’ar’)
18:00 – 22:00           Back home, cattle and goats take their places, family dinner,
                        chatting, listening to the radio, and sleeping

November
Time                    Activities
4:00 – 6:00             People wake up at 4:00 in the night for cutting sorghum (and
                        maize) cane.
6:00 – 12:00            Harvesting sorghum and maize continues
12:00 – 14:00           Lunch
14:00 – 18:00           Harvesting of sorghum and maize continues
18:00 – 22:00           Back home, cattle and goats take their places, family dinner,
                        chatting, listening to the radio, and sleeping

December
Time                       Activities
4:00                       Waking up
4:00 – 18:00               Threshing; carrying straw home (‘migunakh’); food and drinks
                           are served in the threshing place
18:00 – 22:00              Back home, cattle and goats take their places, family dinner,
                           chatting, listening to the radio, and sleeping
Note that soil and water conservation is also undertaken in this month along with the
other activities stated above.

Time                    Activities

18:00 – 22:00           Back home, family chat, family dinner, and sleeping
