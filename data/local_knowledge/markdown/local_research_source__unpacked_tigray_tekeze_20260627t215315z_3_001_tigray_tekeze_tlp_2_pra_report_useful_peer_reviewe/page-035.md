Village Administration

This is the administration at village level comprising six members. It has a chairperson,
vice chairperson and secretary, and four members. The four members are responsible for
different activities within the village. One is responsible for rural development, the
second for security, the third for capacity building and the fourth for organizing the rural
community (popularly know as propaganda). The village community elects all these
members and there is no sex discrimination.
