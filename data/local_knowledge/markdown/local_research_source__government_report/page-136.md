I.1.7.3. Senkeleta
       Each sub-hela has its own elect leader called senkeleta (or helita in
some konso parts). Although a senkeleta appears to be elected by hela
members, his actual recruitment and election is decided by the council of
elders. The basic criterions for election are good behavior and ones
capability to assume responsibility. Elders should believe that the person
in question never lies and never engaged in an extra-marital affair.
Investigations should also prove that he is from a descent family and has
the ability to win the friendship of the society in general and the respect
of members in his hela in particular (Gelgelo Denebo & Tefera Gebabo,
pers. comm.). A good knowledge of Konso tradition is also a requirement.
