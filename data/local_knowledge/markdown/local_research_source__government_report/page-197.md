Dega Diruma II represents stone monuments erected in memory of
individual heroes. The Konso honor their heroes by erecting stone
monuments on their respective tombs. In order to avoid repetitions, the

      Generally, the height of a Daga-hela indicates the fierceness of the
battle. The number of Daga hela in a particular town could help to
estimate the age of a town. However, since some helas did not erect
steles and sometimes steles were removed from their original places, age
estimation by steles could not be reliable. In Karata region the oldest
steles have been erected to the left side and when one move from left to
right the age of the diruma steles gets younger.
