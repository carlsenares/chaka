 IV.8.5. The Moras
         As mentioned earlier Baleda Busso has 16 moras.
         Mora Deracho (5018′44.2′′ latitude and 37025′20.8′′ longitude) is
Busso’s main mora, mora guta. The mora’s pafta is thatched with grass
to the ground. Unlike other paftas, which stand on few poles, this old
style mora has complete wooden wall. Deracho Mora is 30.40m long and
14.50m wide. It has a wide dancing floor, 3 Olayta trees and 7 Daga-
helas.
         Mora Murguto (at coordinate 5018′39.0′′ latitude and 37025′27.7′′
longitude) is the main ritual mora, mora dhawura. This was the first
mora to be established in Busso by the founding fathers of Busso. This
mora is located out side Busso at the eastern side. It is 22.0m long and
61m wide.
