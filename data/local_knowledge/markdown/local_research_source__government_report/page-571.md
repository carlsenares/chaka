(iv)    Visitor/tourism pressure

       Visitors to Konso are usually passers-by who stop for a few hours on
       their way to the Southern Omo region. It is expected that, in the future
       this will change and more visitors may stay longer; as the landscape is
       open and the properties are diverse. There is no any problem
       accommodating tourists. Nevertheless, tourists visiting the traditional
       walled towns put a certain amount of psychological pressure on the
       elderly community members.

       From the current experience, the walled towns can accommodate up to
       four groups of 10 to 15 guided visitors at a time. This number could be
       accommodated     without   adverse   effects   provided   that   the   tourist
       experience is aided by trained local guides.
