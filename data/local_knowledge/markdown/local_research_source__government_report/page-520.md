C.    Mora

In the traditional walled towns, cultural spaces called Mora are located at the
center of the main central enclosure and at different locations within the
walls, and some times, out side the walls. Paths from the all gates lead to
these Moras. The individual walled town (Paleta) has up to 17 Moras which
are connected to one other by foot paths.

In the Kolme and Doha areas, where there are no walled towns, the Moras
are located in central spaces that are easy to access for all, and sometimes
on major trails.

The Moras retain an important and central role in the life of the Konso. In the
walled towns, they have, in most cases specially designed one story grass
thatched houses, called Pafta. The Pafta are constructed on stone platform
