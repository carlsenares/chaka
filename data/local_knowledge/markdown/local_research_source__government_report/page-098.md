7. c. Form and date of most recent records of property

The most recent records of the cultural properties were made between
October 2007 and January 2008.

These records include:

For the walled towns-

- Relevant quantitative and qualitative data relative to the stone walls of
 traditional towns: height, thickness, rock type, rock dimensions at certain
 intervals; gates and their cultural purpose; state of condition of the walls
 are documented using GPS and data sheet prepared for these purpose.

- The cultural spaces (Mora) and cultural objects within the walls such as the
 Pafta (common houses), the generation trees (ulahita), and other objects of
 cultural significance are equally documented.

- All the paths that connect the cultural spaces within the towns are tracked.
