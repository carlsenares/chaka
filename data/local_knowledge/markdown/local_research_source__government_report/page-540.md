Fig. 21 - The Bamale sacred forest covers an area of 105,338m.
            square area. It is located within the Dokatu Paleta.

The Kufa is a traditionally protected ritual forest located at 5.1744 north and
37.23 East, within the Mecheke Paleta. The Kufa sacred forest covers an area
of 45,066 m. square area.

Like the Bamale and Kala, Kufa is the name of the ritual leader and the
forest. The Poqola (ritual leader) live near the forest. There are ancient graves
of ancestors of the present Poqola at the center of the forest. The grave
marker Wakas are decayed due to age. The dirt road leading to Mecheke cuts
through the forest. There are two Hardas (ponds) located in the forest.
