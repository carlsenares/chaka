15.    Registration, Study, Research and Promotion of Heritage
            1. The Konso Cultural Landscape shall, for purposes of identification, inspection,

            2. Details of operation shall be according to directives to be issued by the

                research on Konso Cultural Landscape Heritage based on permits issued by the
                concerned offices.
