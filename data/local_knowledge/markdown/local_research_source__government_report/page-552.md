3.b   Proposed Statement of Outstanding Universal Value

The Konso Cultural landscape located in the southern part of Ethiopia
demonstrates 500 years of man / environment relationship and has been
referred to by ICOMOS as “extraordinary landscape”.       Konso is a complex
cultural landscape that has and continues to attract attention of world
scholars due to its amalgamation of unique architecture, land use, space
planning and management, resource mobilization, ritual practices, all
combined into one in an otherwise hostile environment.
