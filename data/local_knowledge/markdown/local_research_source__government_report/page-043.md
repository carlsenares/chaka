Fig. 20- The Bamale sacred forest covers an area of 105,338m. square area. It
         is located within the Dokatu Paleta.

- Kufa is a traditionally protected ritual forest located within the Mecheke
 Paleta. The Kufa sacred forest covers an area of 45,066 m. square area.

 Like the Bamale and Kala, Kufa is the name of the ritual leader and the
 forest. The Poqola (ritual leader) live near the forest. There are ancient
 graves of ancestors of the present Poqola at the center of the forest. The
 grave marker Wakas are decayed due to age. The dirt road leading to
 Mecheke cuts through the forest. There are two Hardas (ponds) located in
 the forest.
