IV.2.3. Gates and Moras
      Gocha has five main gates and two smaller openings, Fulas. The
main sacred ritual gate is Kara Baleda located at 5020′24.1′′ latitude and
37023′58.8′′ longitude. This gate used to have big wooden poles and an
arc, which, however, are currently decayed.

Moras of Gocha
      Gocha has six moras, five within the walled town and one out side,
at the western vicinity of the town. Only two of Gocha’s moras, Mora
Katayta and Mora Tubaya, have pafta. One mora, Mora Kasara, used to
have a pafta in the past which now is decayed.
