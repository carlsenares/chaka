III.4.3. Organization with in the towns
a - Kanta (ward) organization
      Each town has a “ward” (Kanta) system which enables the
smooth functioning of the traditional political and socio-economic
system. Each individual community member belongs to a certain
Kanta.    The kanta system is not bounded by the walls and
members of a certain Kanta can be residents of any of the walls
(See maps in Chapter IV). Membership to a certain Kanta is
purely a practical socio-economic classification.
      Each kanta is divided into kanta-ibita, sub-wards, and each kanta-
ibita is constituted by tightly crowded individual household compounds.
