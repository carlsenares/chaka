CHAPTER I
            Konso: Land, People and Heritage

I.1. General Introduction of the Konso
   I.1.1 Konso Location
      Konso is located 600 Kms. south of Addis Ababa. The nominated
core area proposed lies between 5016’15” and 5021′20′′N and 37020′15′′
and 370 26’49′′ E. The Konso people inhabit in the Konso Special Woreda
within South Nations, Nationalities and Peoples’ Regional State. Konso
Special Woreda (administrative district) covers an area of 2354.3 km2. It
is bordered by Darashe, Amaro and Burji Special Woredas and Oromia
Regional State and Debub Omo Zones, in the north, northeast, east,
south and west respectively.
