E. Mora Koptale
       Mora Koptale is located in Lower Dhokato. It belongs to Kanta
Odantita and is the main mora of Parguta section of Lower Dhokato.
Power transfer discourses by each generation grade group of the three
sister towns take place at this mora. After the power transfer decision is
made, the senqeleta, leader of the generation grade, takes the power
symbolizing stick from the retiring generation grade at this mora.
       During this power transfer ceremony there is a ritual called dhota.
For dhota ritual a bull is suspended high on four logs carried by the
power taking generation members and is stabbed at its nape from
underneath by a ritual spear, orana, by member of a ritual family named
Bamale. This bull has to be red/brown in color and should be very
