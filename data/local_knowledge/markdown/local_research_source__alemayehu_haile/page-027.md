      2.6. Conceptual Framework
The measures of adoption in this study are the actual physical presence of conservation
structures on farmers' plot of land. Hence, farmers were asked whether conservation
structures are done in their field or not and still exist in each plot of land. In this study
adopters are fanners who have a plot of land on which conservation structures have been
put up. Thus, the dependent variable is a function of demographic, socio-economic,
institutional and physical factors.
