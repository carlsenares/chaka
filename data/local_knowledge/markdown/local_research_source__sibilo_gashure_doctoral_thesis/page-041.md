3.1. The study area descriptions

3.1.1. Location
Intensively terraced Konso Cultural Landscapes are found in Ethiopia’s southern Rift
Valley region (ARCCH, 2009). Astronomically, it is located between 5° 12’−5° 40’ N
and 37° 01’−37° 43’ E (Figure 1). It covers an area of 2273.38 km2. The cultural
landscape exemplifies the spectacular engineering ingenuity of humans in their struggle
to survive in harsh environments. Although oral traditions claim that Konso cultural
landscape sculpture has existed for 400 years as part of ongoing cultural practices to tame
the hostile environment for human habitation, archaeological confirmation has yet to be
obtained.
