                     SURVEY QUESTIONNAIRE FOR HOUSEHOLDS

The purpose of this questionnaire is to gather data on the long-term dynamics of land use/ land
cover and its impact on erosion rate, soil quality, and soil organic carbon stocks from farm
households in Bururi watershed of Oromia region, Western Ethiopia.
