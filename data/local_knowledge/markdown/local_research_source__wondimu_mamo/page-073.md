In RUSLE model, the cover management (C-factor) accounts for how land use and its
management cause soil erosion (Kinnell, 2010). The various land use and land cover types have
different role in controlling soil erosion. For instance, land covered by better vegetation protects
the soil against erosion (Morgan, 1977) (and in contrary vegetation removal for different
purposes leads to the depletion of soil organic matter (SOM) and increase soil erosion risk. To
determine the C-factors in the present study, the LULC maps of 1957, 1987, and 2018 were used
and this was done to examine effects of LULC dynamics on soil erosion rate.
