 Dependent Variable

The dependent variable in this study was the adoption of a particular land management option.
The land management types specifically implemented in the study area were grouped into four
categories (physical structures, agronomic, vegetative/biological, and management measures)
and a respondent is considered as an adopter for one category if he/she implemented one of land
management types mentioned in that particular category (Table 3.8). Therefore, a householder
classified as an adopter in one land management category might be a non-adopter in another
category, and vice versa. The description of the dependent variables is given in Table 3.8.

     Table 3.8 Description of Dependent Variables (WOCAT, 2007; Babalola and Olayemi, 2013)

                               soil/stone bund, check dam, and terraces (1 if implemented, 0
