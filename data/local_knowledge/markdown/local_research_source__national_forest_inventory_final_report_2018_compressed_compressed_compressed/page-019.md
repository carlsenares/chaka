• The following actions are recommended in order for Ethiopia to enhance its NFI:
   o Formal approval of the new national forest definition;
   o Assign permanent teams to the NFI in order to create specialized and
     professional teams;
   o Take into account the change in the national forest definition in order to
     harmonize the data analysis and reporting consistently;
   o Decentralize field operations so that they are better coordinated technically in
     order to secure faster and more efficient field data collection phase;
   o Establish clear protocols for data flow between the NFI levels (i.e. operators);
   o Reinforce links between remote sensing analysis, inventory planning and field
     data collection;
   o Use NFI field data as validation of RS results (forest cover/change detection analysis);
   o Conduct further training and supervision to reduce the number of measurement
     errors and improve the QA/QC procedures;
   o Include a botanist in each team;
   o Reinforce links with the academia by allowing the research teams to access the
     NFI data and to improve the quality of the variables collected. This will yield an
     increased number of topical publications, thesis, and books that can emphasize
     the importance, complexity and uniqueness of the Ethiopian forests.
