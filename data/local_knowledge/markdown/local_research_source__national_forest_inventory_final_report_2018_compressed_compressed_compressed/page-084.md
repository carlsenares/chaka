3.11               Forest disturbance and
                   health status
In NFI data about the types of human disturbances affecting the forest or other wooded
land were collected. Based on the NFI data, 65.8% of forest lands are somehow
disturbed, and 58.8% of OWLs are disturbed by human activity. These are rather high
figures but typical to countries with high population pressure on forest resources. In
forest land the main disturbance is attributed to the encroachment by agriculture.
