along the DBH classes than in the other land types, and only the 37% of it is stored in
the bigger diameter classes (i.e. trees DBH ≥ 35 cm). The general overview suggests a
possibility to increase more rational management planning in the forest plantations,
and at the same time there are possibilities to support silvicultural practices in the
Natural regenerated forests, at least areas with low stockings.

3.7                  Biomass and carbon stocks
The AGB estimates have been calculated by using allometric equations, the BGB as
fraction of the ABG. The carbon fraction of the biomass is obtained by using default
values. The deadwood fraction is calculated separately. The carbon in litter using the
measurement collected in the field is described in a separate report together with the
soil organic carbon (SOC) estimates.
