3.2.4                   Tree density by height classes

Mean tree density by 5cm range tree height classes show that the highest density of
trees (DBH≥10cm) is in the height class 10-14.9m in Forest and OWL, and in the class
5-9.9m in Other Land.

The results by biomes show that the highest proportion of trees taller than 20m in
Forest are found in Moist Afromontane biome (29% of trees), whereas Other Wooded
Land contains relatively few trees taller than 20m.
