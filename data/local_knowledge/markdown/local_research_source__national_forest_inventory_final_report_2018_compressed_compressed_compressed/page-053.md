3.1.1             Forest and non-forest areas
The area estimates derived from the NFI and used to calculate the per hectare statistics, show
a forest area of 6,520,900 ha, equal to 5.7% of the country’s area, and consisting of natural
forests (6,147,500 ha) and plantations (373,400 ha). The woodland area is estimated to cover
a total of 57,458,700 ha, corresponding to 49.9% of the total land area.

The other two FRA classes, Other Land and Inland Water, occupy 50,703,900 ha (44.1% of the
total country area) and 360,400 ha (0.3%), respectively. The complete area results are
presented in Appendix A2.
