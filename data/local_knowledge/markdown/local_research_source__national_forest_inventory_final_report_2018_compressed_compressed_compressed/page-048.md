2.9                Biomass models
Several Biomass models39 have been developed for Ethiopia (at least 6440). However, all of
the models identified are species-specific (for Dichrostachys cinerea, Euclea shimperi,
Eucalyptus camadulensis, Eucalyptus globulus, Grewia bicolor and Otostegia integrifolia)
and site-specific (National Regional State of Tigray, Ker Jarso, Jufi, Ethiopian Heritage
Forestry Park, Weldeab Ager, Addis Abeba, Munessa Shashamane).
In the absence of applicable biomass models for every Ethiopian ecosystem/biome
consistent with international requirements, the choice between the models has been
restricted to the pan-tropical model of Chave et al. (2014).
