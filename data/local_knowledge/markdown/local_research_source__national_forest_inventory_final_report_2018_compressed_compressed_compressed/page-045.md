because the NFI design was based on five strata that did not perfectly overlap with the

In order to ensure consistency of the estimates, a robust statistical procedure was
applied based on the method described by Sarndal et al. (1992).
The first step implies to use of a ratio estimator, defined by the ratio between the
variable y (tree variable) and the variable x (hectares of forest). Both y and x have been
measured for each plot. The index d, which in statistical literature is referred to as
a”domain”, refers to the biomes.The NFI design is a stratified sampling therefore each
stratum has a different sampling intensity defined by the inclusion probability πk (of
each plot). The πk has been computed by dividing the number of hectares sampled in
each stratum by the total area of the strata (when the sampling intensity is higher,
inclusion probability is higher).
