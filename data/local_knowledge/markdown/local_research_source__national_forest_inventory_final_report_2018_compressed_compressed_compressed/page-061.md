or equal to 1.3 m and DBH less than 10 cm. In natural regenerated forest the highest
mean density of saplings is located in Bamboo forest (4,759 trees ha −1), followed by
Evergreen and Deciduous forest types, where the mean densities are 3,553 and 2,886,
respectively.

3.3.2             Regeneration count by biomes

The number of trees per hectare by biomes in regeneration data shows the highest
stocking in the Dry Afromontaine biome (6,423), followed by the biome identified as
‘Other’ (5,382, this biome is a very small by area and it falls dominantly into non-
permanent rivers). The third highest density is found in the Acacia Commiphora biome
(4,481).
