3.4.1                Basal area by FRA classes and forest type

The basal area by FRA classes shows a similar pattern to tree density and volume. The
mean basal area in Ethiopian forest is 13.3 m2 ha−1, and 1.7 m2 ha−1 in Other Wooded Land.
Table 3.11 shows that evergreen forest has the highest basal area (15.6 m2 ha−1),
followed closely by coniferous planted forests (14.8 m2 ha−1).
