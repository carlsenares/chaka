3 Results
The NFI design was based on a stratification scheme described in the methodology
section (Chapter 2) and the data collection followed that approach. These strata have
then been replaced in Ethiopia with biomes for reporting purposes. Therefore, in this
document the results have been reported at the country level and per biomes.
The reporting levels regarding the vegetation/land use categories follow the
methodology described in the NFI Field Manual, i.e. containing FRA categories (Level1),
Major LUCC types (Level2) and LUCC (Level3). In order to meet the national
information needs of regional states, the data has been also analyzed and reported also
byregions (in Appendices).
NFI consisted of 539 accessible SUs (containing a total of 2,077 plots, and 2,853 plot
cover sections). Remaining 88 SUs have been identified as inaccessible due to different
factors including excessive remoteness, topography and temporary security problems.
A supplementary crosscheck of all the inaccessible SUs was conducted using Google
Collect Earth survey data. After this analysis, for the SUs classified as possibly
accessible, a new attempt to reach the sites was accomplished.
Finally, 49,829 trees and 2029 stumps were recorded and analyzed in NFI and a
cumulative plot area of 1,029 ha was surveyed by the field crews during approximately
27 months (Table 3.1).

3.1                Area results
 Area results reported in this section have been calculated by analyzing LULC data
 observed and recorded in the field sample plots. Thus, the resulting figures are
 different from the maps and estimates derived from the remote sensing (RS) material.
 The area data derived from the field observations were used to obtain the per hectare
 statistics which can be multiplied by the official area estimates to obtain total
 estimates.
Therefore, the results shared in this report are presented as per hectare statistics and
proportions between the different LULC types. The results can be used to calculate
proportions between the LULC, but cannot substitute the country level area estimates
obtained with RS analysis. The official area estimates of RS analysis are not reported
here.
