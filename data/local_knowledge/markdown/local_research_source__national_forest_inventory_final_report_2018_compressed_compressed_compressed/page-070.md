The stump contribution to the AGB, BGB and carbon is presented in Table 3.21. Stumps
with diameter of more or equal to 10 cm were recorded in Forest Land. In general,
stumps represent a minor part of forest biomass and carbon in Ethiopian forests and
lands outside of forests. The estimated biomass of stumps is approximately 1.1 t ha−1 in
both Major LUCC types in the forest land.

type.
