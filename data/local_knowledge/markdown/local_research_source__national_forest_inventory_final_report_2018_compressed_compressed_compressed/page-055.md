3.1.2           Land cover area by biomes

In the following figures (Fig. 3.5–3.8), area proportions are presented by the Major
LUCC types in each biome45. There are total of four biomes in Ethiopia (cf. Chapter 2). The
Acacia- Commiphora biome area is dominantly covered by Other Wooded Land, 73.1%
of the total area in the biome, followed by Natural Other Land (15.7%). In Combretum-
Terminalia, the dominating Major LUCC class is Other Land - Cultivated (42.5%),
followed by Other Land – Natural (8.9%) and Natural regenerated forest (8.3%). The
area proportions observed in the Dry Afromontane biome shows a Cultivated Other
Land occupies 61.6% of the area, followed by Other Land – Natural (18.7%) and Other
Wooded Land (11.2%). The Moist Afromontane is the biome that is covered by Other
land - Cultivated (48.2%), followed by the Natural regenerated forest (26.3%).
