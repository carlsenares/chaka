Household Level Vulnerability


The factor score of the first principal component for entire observation was used to construct
vulnerability index at household level. The value ranges from -1.72 for household characterized
as highly vulnerable to +2.12 for least vulnerable among the observations. The result shows that
the majority of households fall within the moderately vulnerable category in the watershed
followed by high vulnerability status (Table 4.2).
