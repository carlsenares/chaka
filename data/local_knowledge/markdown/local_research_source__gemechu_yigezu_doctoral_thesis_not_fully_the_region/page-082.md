The watershed is characterized by moderate and high rainfall concentration with considerable

moderate to extreme drought in few years in all agro-ecologies (Table 3b). It revealed that no
drought situations (SRA ≥ -0.84) were observed for most of years in the climatologic time in
the watershed. The 1986 was the year of extreme drought (SRA < -1.65) for both woinadega
