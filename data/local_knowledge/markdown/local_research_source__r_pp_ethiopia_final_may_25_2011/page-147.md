Coordination
A high-level national coordination and cooperation mechanism should be established to:
    o    Link forest carbon MRV system to the national policy for REDD+
    o    Specify and oversee roles, responsibilities and co-benefits, and other monitoring
         efforts
A REDD steering committee is already established and will be responsible for the coordination
task as well. FEPA and representatives of Regional EPAs will play key roles in this steering
committee regarding the MRV system. If and when a dedicated federal agency for forestry is
created, it will take the active leadership from EPA, which will then continue with its
regulatory role.
