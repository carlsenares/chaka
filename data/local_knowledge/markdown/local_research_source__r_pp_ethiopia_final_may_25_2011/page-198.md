Level 1: Federal Democratic Republic of Ethiopia
Level 2: Kililoch
Ethiopia is divided into 9 administrative regions called kililoch (singular – kilil) or Regional
States. The nine regions are:
     Afar
     Amhara
     Benishangul-Gumuz
     Gambella
     Harari
     Oromia
     Somali
     Southern Nations, Nationalities, and Peoples Region (SNNP)
     Tigray
The regional divisions have substituted the thirteen former provinces, to which still is being
referred when describing locations. As described in a previous post, the government has
decentralized the implementation of land management to the Regional States in 1997.
