Institutional set-up
As Ethiopia moves into the readiness phase, it must establish the organizational capacity to
operate a national and/or sub national forest carbon MRV programme efficiently and
sustainably. The requirements for a national institutional framework for MRV are (i)
coordination, (ii) monitoring, (iii) reporting and (iv) verification.
