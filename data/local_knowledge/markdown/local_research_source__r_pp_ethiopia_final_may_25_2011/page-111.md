Agriculture, forestry and other land uses (AFOLU) definition for Ethiopia
Though in the present context Ethiopia‘s aim is REDD+, its overall aim is to include all its
economic sectors so as to achieve its 0% emission target by 2025. Therefore, REDD+ has to be
seen as a component part of this overall aim.
Ethiopia defines four classes of forests, which may be subdivided again according to crown
cover or stem density (WBISPP, 2004) (Table 20). There has not as yet been a measurement of
all its grasslands and wetlands. This has to be done in the next few years. The extent of its
agricultural land, though varying from year to year, is assessed annually by the Statistics
Agency.
