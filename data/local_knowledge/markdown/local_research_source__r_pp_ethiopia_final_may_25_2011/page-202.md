The FAO forest resource assessment 2010 Ethiopia (FRA 2010) is based on WBISPP 2004 data.
For the assessment the WBISPP (2004) data is calibrated with a factor of 0.96 regarding the
total country area and reclassified: forest, high woodland area and plantations figures are
combined to the FRA 2010 class forest. Low woodland and shrub land figures are combined to
the class ―other wooded land‖ (OWL). The land cover change trends observed 1990-2000 have
been extrapolated linearly to estimate 2010 land cover in the FRA 2010.
