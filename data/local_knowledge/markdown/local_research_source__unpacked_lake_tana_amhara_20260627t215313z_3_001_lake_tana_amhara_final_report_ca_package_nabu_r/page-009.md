2.2 Focus group discussions

undertaken, focus group discussions (FGDs) were held at North Achefer (figure 2), Bahirdar

discussions were undertaken on issues related to cropping patterns, crop rotation, crop residue
management, livestock keeping and feed sources, existing land management and tillage
practices, etc. The outcome of the FGDs was used to develop a full-blown questionnaire.
