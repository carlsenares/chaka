4.7 Farmer’s perception on climate change

Although there had been more hot days and less rainfall, all respondents, including the farmers,
were aware of the effects of climate change. The decomposing rains, crop drying, sorghum, and
maize disease were mentioned by the respondents as signs of a changing climate. Additionally,
every respondent linked human activity to climate change, citing the careless falling of trees and
the removal of vegetation to make way for human activity.
