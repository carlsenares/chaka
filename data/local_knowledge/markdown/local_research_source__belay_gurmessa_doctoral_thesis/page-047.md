better able to adjust to changing climate conditions (Deressa et al. 2009).

There is frequently a land shortage in the district. Among the primary causes is the startling rate
at which population density is growing and the fragmentation of land brought about by large
household numbers of children. Melaku (2016) reported that the Oromo people refer to their land
as "Dachee haadha Margoo," which translates to "mother earth" in English. According to Oromo
wisdom, the mother earth is revered and appreciated. We all die and pass away; not only do
humans pass away, but also plant and animal species do as well. She raises her offspring in the
