BIOGRAPHICAL SKETCH

The author was born in wine-Rogge kebele Gindeberat district, West Shewa, Oromia Regional
State of Ethiopia, on December 19, 1979 E.C., to his mother Kifle Tirge and his father Gurmessa
Tafa. He attended his elementary education in Abuye Rogge elementary school. He also attended
his junior education at Kachis secondary school. Then, he joined Ginchi Comprehensive
Secondary School and completed his secondary education in 1997 E.C. In 1998 E.C., the author
joined Bahir Dar University and graduated with a BA degree in geography in July 2000 E.C.
After his graduation, he was employed in Chiro town. Meanwhile, he joined the School of
Graduate Studies of AAU in August 2009 E.C. with an MA degree in geography.
