the impact of seasonal migration engagement on SLM adoption.
PSM is widely recognized in impact evaluation for creating comparable groups by balancing
observed covariates. In our study, it enables us to estimate the causal effect by comparing the
differences in SLM adoption intensity between matched migrant and non-migrant households.
The propensity score, P(X), summarizes these characteristics and represents the probability of
a household participating in migration, conditional on observed covariates X:
P(X) = Pr [D=1∣X]=E[D∣X]                                                                 (7.1)
