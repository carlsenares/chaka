in the context of the Dry Land of upper Tekeze basin, Waghimra zone. Thus, it is crucial to
examine how land management is sustained under changing land use/cover managements, high
soil erosion rates, and chronic drought affected and high seasonal labor migration area of the
dry land of upper Tekeze basin. It is important to investigate the challenge and opportunities
of land management in the context where the four interrelated themes— land use/cover
changes, drought, land degradation by erosion, and rural households’ seasonal out-migration—
act as interconnected phenomena creating a particular distinct scenario.
