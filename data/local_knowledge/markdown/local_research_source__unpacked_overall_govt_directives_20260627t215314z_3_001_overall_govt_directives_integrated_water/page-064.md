5.4.4 Groundwater and spring water (water level, yield, and quality)
Effects of watershed management include groundwater recharge, enhanced spring discharge, and
changes in water quality. To quantify impact, it is necessary to evaluate both groundwater and spring
water before and after interventions. Regular monitoring requires groundwater measurements (static
water level and quality of groundwater) and spring water measurements (discharge rate and water quality).
The parameters to be evaluated appear in Table 32.
