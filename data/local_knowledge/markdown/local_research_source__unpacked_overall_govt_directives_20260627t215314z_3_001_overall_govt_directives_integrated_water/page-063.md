5.4.3 Evaluating ex situ water harvesting (design, type, storage, functionality)
The design and performance evaluations of water harvesting structures require (a) proper evaluation of
the amount of available water resources (surface water and groundwater), (b) selection of appropriate
water harvesting/development technology that suits the local context, (c) selection of type of water
resources, and (d) evaluation of the performance/functionality of the structures (Table 31).
