5.4.9 Community participation in monitoring and impact assessment
      of integrated watershed management
An appraisal of different watershed technologies and interventions in terms of adoption and effectiveness
vis-à-vis local interests must be considered in the standardization. It must consider not only the technological
means involved but also the approaches that are intended to facilitate successful implementation and
adaptation of technologies. In evaluating problems, stakeholders such as farmers must be invited to
take part in a broad participatory process to understand and analyze problems and express and evaluate
their needs, interests, and aims. To enhance action-oriented research and development, continuous
