Methodological issues

Direct N2O emission from Managed Soils was calculated using Tier 1 methodology, Equation 11.1 from
the 2006 IPCC Guideline and default emission factor was used from the 2006 IPCC Guideline.

Fraction of N in Synthetic fertilizer


Nitrous oxide emission from synthetic fertilizer was estimate based on the amount of N in synthetic fertilizer
that is annually used in the country. Data on the annual consumption of synthetic fertilizers was obtained
from the annual Farm Management practice Report published by the Ethiopian Central Statistics service
from the year 2018-2020.
