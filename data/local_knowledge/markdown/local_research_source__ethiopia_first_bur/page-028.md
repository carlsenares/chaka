which account for approximately 87% of the total primary energy use (Tiruye, 2021).

Ethiopia aims to generate foreign exchange by fully utilizing its potential for electricity production,
particularly from hydropower plants. The substantial electricity generation from hydropower, including
projects like the Ethiopian Renaissance Dam, will also contribute to the economic stability of the region.
