  4.7 Overview of GHG Emissions
Ethiopia’s total emissions were estimated at 350,843.9 GgCO2eq in 2018 and 262,384.50 GgCO2eq in 2020.
The AFOLU sector was the most significant source of emissions for the three gasses (i.e., CO2, CH4 and N2O),
accountingfor 82.5% of the total emissions in the year 2020 (Figure 4-1). The energy sector was the second most
